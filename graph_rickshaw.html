    • Prof. (Dr.) J.P. Singh
    Director

    Dr. J. P. Singh is a known academician and researcher in the field of Management Sciences. He is having a vast career spanning over more than 37 years. After completing his M.Sc. in Statistics from Meerut University, he joined CCS Haryana Agricultural University, Hisar as Asst. Scientist/Asst. Professor in September 1976. He did his Ph.D. in Statistics from H.A.U. Hisar, Haryana in year 1987 and reached to the level of professor at the young age of 43 years. He also has successfully completed One Year Faculty Development Programme in Management from Indian Institute of Management, Ahmedabad.

    He worked with Shri Mata Vaishno Devi University, Katra, J&K, India as Dean, College of Management from August 2007 to August 2009. After retirement from CCS Haryana Agricultural University, he joined Shri Venkateshwara University, Gajraula, Amroha, U.P. as Professor & Dean in School of Management and held various important positions like Director of Research & Planning, Dean of School of Engineering etc.

    His areas of interest are Applied Econometrics, Business Environment, Business Statistics & Mathematics, Entrepreneurship, Operation Research Methods, Production and Operations Management and Research Methodology etc. He is the receipt of many National and International fellowships and grants such as ICAR Senior Fellowship, International Biometric Society Travel Grant, Australian International Development Assistance Bureau etc. He has published more than 50 research articles in various National and International Publications.

    He has been a member of various professional societies such as Indian Society of Agricultural Economics (Life Member), Indian Society of Agricultural Statistics (Life Member), Indian Society of Information Theory and Applications (Life Member), International Biometric Society, New Zealand Statistical Association etc. He has organized various Training & Development Programmes and has been associated with various prestigious Universities of India as Expert Speaker.
